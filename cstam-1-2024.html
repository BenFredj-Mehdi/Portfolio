CSTAM 1.0 — AI & Gaming Edition

IEEE ESPRIT Student Branch, Laico Hammamet Hotel November 16 – November 17, 2024

Participating in the first edition of CSTAM was a unique and energizing experience focused on the intersection of artificial intelligence and gaming. The event created a strong space for innovation, technical discovery, and exchange between students and professionals.

Through workshops, expert talks, and panel sessions, we explored how AI is reshaping game design, player interaction, and future opportunities in the gaming ecosystem. Beyond the technical content, the congress offered valuable networking moments with participants from IEEE communities across Tunisia.

I am thankful to the organizing team for delivering such a high-quality edition, and to the IEEE TEK-UP Student Branch team for the support and great spirit throughout the event. This edition strengthened my motivation to keep learning and building in this space.
