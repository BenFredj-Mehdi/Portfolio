TSYP 13.0 — IEEE Tunisian Student & Young Professional Congress

Medina Congress Center, Yasmine Hammamet December 22 – December 24, 2025

Participating in TSYP 13.0 was a motivating and enriching experience. The congress brought together driven students and young professionals from all over Tunisia, creating a dynamic environment centered on learning, exchange, and collaboration.

I was especially inspired by the technical sessions, including the AWS AI Fundamentals workshop led by Ms. Khouloud Ghabi. The event strengthened my commitment to come back even more prepared next year, with the goal of representing IEEE TEK-UP Student Branch through a strong project.

I’m grateful to IEEE ENIS Student Branch for delivering such a well-organized edition, and I wish IEEE INSAT Student Branch the very best for TSYP 14. Many thanks as well to TEK-UP University of Digital Sciences, IEEE TEK-UP Student Branch, and Mr. Khaled Jerbi for their support.
