Building Bridges Between Tunisia and Sweden

STHK, Embassy of Sweden in Tunisia & MSB November 27, 2025

Participating in this conference was a valuable opportunity to engage with Tunisian and Swedish academics, professionals, and business leaders in a space dedicated to cooperation and shared progress.

The discussions highlighted practical paths for stronger partnerships between universities and companies, joint research initiatives, student mobility, and internship opportunities. The new cooperation agreement between SMU/MedTech and Mälardalens universitet (MDU) stood out as a strong signal of long-term collaboration.

The event offered both strategic insight and meaningful networking, and it reinforced the importance of building cross-border bridges to support innovation and student development.
