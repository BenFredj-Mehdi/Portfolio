CyberSphere Congress 6.0

Engineers Spark - ISET'COM x Securinets April 25 – April 27, 2025

Taking part in CyberSphere Congress was an intense and highly rewarding experience. The event gathered a wide cybersecurity community and offered a strong technical program that balanced competition, practical learning, and expert guidance.

From HackBack challenges and beginner/advanced CTF tracks to the Attack vs Defense format, every part of the congress pushed participants to think faster, adapt, and collaborate under pressure. The workshops and talks added valuable real-world insight from experienced professionals.

This experience strengthened my motivation in cybersecurity and reminded me how powerful community-driven events can be for growth. Huge thanks to the organizing teams and everyone who contributed to such a well-executed edition.
