Digital Future 6 — Tunisian German Academic Congress

TEK-UP University & Hochschule Schmalkalden, Sousse November 20 – November 25, 2024

Taking part in the 6th edition of Digital Future was a memorable international academic experience. Under the Data Literacy theme, the congress offered rich discussions on how data-driven thinking and AI are transforming education, research, and professional practice.

Throughout the week, Tunisian and German professors delivered insightful sessions that connected theory with real-world applications. Beyond the technical content, collaborating with students from both countries made the event especially valuable and highlighted the strength of cross-cultural exchange.

I am deeply grateful to all organizers and partners for creating this opportunity, and especially thankful for the leadership and support that made such international collaboration possible. This experience reinforced my motivation to keep building in technology and innovation.
