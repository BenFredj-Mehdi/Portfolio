CSTAM 2.0 — IEEE CS Tunisian Annual Meeting

Barceló Concorde Green Park Palace, Sousse November 22 – November 23, 2025

Taking part in CSTAM 2.0 was an energizing and valuable experience. The event offered strong technical content, especially around cloud computing, while also creating a great space to connect with motivated students and professionals from different regions of Tunisia.

I appreciated the quality of the sessions and the collaborative spirit throughout the meeting. It was an excellent opportunity to strengthen both technical knowledge and professional relationships in the local IEEE community.

My sincere thanks to IEEE ENICarthage Student Branch and all organizers for delivering such a smooth and impactful edition, as well as to IEEE Computer Society Tunisia Chapter, IEEE CS TEK-UP Student Branch Chapter, and IEEE TEK-UP Student Branch for their continuous support.
