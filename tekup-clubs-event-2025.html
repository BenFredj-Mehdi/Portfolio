TEK-UP University Clubs Event — 2nd Edition

Sol Azur Hotel, Hammamet May 17 – May 18, 2025

Participating in this second edition of the TEK-UP Clubs Event was a proud and meaningful experience. Over two days, active student club members came together to collaborate, share ideas, and exchange perspectives in a highly engaging atmosphere.

One of the strongest moments of the event was the themed competition on how university clubs can support the Palestinian cause. Through teamwork and creative thinking, our group proposed practical awareness and solidarity initiatives, and we were honored to earn first place.

I am grateful to TEK-UP University of Digital Sciences, Mr. Khaled Jerbi, and all organizers for creating this impactful space. Events like this highlight how student communities can lead positive action and meaningful change.
