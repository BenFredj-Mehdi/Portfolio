CyberHorizon 2.0

SecuriNets FST April 3 – April 5, 2026

Participating in CyberHorizon 2.0 at Sol Azur Hammamet was an excellent practical learning experience and a major motivation boost in my cybersecurity journey.

Over three intensive days, I worked on hands-on cybersecurity challenges that pushed me to think faster, analyze deeper, and improve my approach to problem-solving under pressure.

Beyond the technical side, the event offered a strong collaborative atmosphere where exchanging ideas with skilled and passionate participants helped me learn new perspectives and methods.

I am grateful to SecuriNets FST and SecuriNets TEK-UP for organizing this valuable event, to TEK-UP University of Digital Sciences for supporting these opportunities, and to Mr. Khaled Jerbi for his continuous guidance and encouragement.

Experiences like this continue to strengthen my technical skills and reinforce my commitment to continuous learning in cybersecurity.
