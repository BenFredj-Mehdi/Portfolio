AINS 3.0 — Artificial Intelligence National Summit

IEEE Computer Society INSAT SBC, Sousse June 13 – June 15, 2025

Participating in AINS 3.0 was an exciting milestone in my learning path. The summit brought together motivated students, engineers, and tech enthusiasts from across Tunisia to discuss practical AI trends and future opportunities.

Through talks and exchanges, I explored topics such as Generative AI, NLP, Edge AI, and MLOps while connecting with professionals and peers who are actively building in the field. The collaborative atmosphere made every session both technical and inspiring.

This event gave me strong momentum to continue growing my AI skills and move toward real-world AI projects. Many thanks to IEEE Computer Society INSAT SBC and all organizers for delivering such a high-impact edition.
